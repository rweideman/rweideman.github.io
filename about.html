About

Who I Am

I am an Electrical and Computer Engineering student with a strong interest in hardware design, embedded systems, and biotechnology-focused applications. My goal is to build technologies that solve meaningful problems through thoughtful engineering, practical design, and careful testing.

My academic and project experience has helped me develop a foundation in circuit design, PCB layout, prototyping, data processing, and system-level problem solving. I enjoy the process of taking an idea from concept to implementation and refining it until it works reliably and clearly serves its purpose.

Academic Interests

My interests include biomedical devices, signal processing, hardware development, and the role of electronics in improving healthcare and research tools. I am especially drawn to projects that combine technical precision with real-world impact.

Technical Experience

Through coursework, projects, and technical work, I have developed experience in PCB design, circuit testing, board mapping, data analysis, and project collaboration. I am interested in continuing to grow in environments where I can contribute both technically and creatively.
